TASK 3: Typography Practice

This typography design is built around the motivational phrase “Battle Your Fears”, using strong visual hierarchy and contrast to emphasize courage and determination.
The dark background enhances readability and creates a dramatic, powerful atmosphere, while the combination of white and orange colors conveys strength, energy, and confidence. The overlapping arrangement of the text adds depth and visual interest, directing the viewer's attention toward the central message.

       DESIGN LOGIC:
- Bold sans-serif typography for impact and readability.
- Orange outlined text to create emphasis and contrast.
- Distressed texture representing struggle and resilience.
- Dark background for a modern and motivational aesthetic.
- Layered composition that creates depth and visual balance.
